## Question
How do you help junior or mid-level engineers improve their work?
Can you give a concrete example?

## What this tests
- Ability to multiply team effectiveness
- Willingness to invest in others
- Communication clarity

## Acceptable answers
- Mentions PR reviews, pairing, design discussions
- Gives a specific example
- Focuses on improving thinking, not just fixing code

## Red flags
- “I prefer working alone”
- No examples
- Only gives instructions, not guidance
