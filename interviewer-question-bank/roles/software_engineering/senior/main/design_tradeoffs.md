## Question
Tell me about a system or feature where you were involved in design decisions.
What trade-offs did you make, and why?

## What this tests
- Ability to reason about alternatives
- Understanding of constraints (scale, time, team, legacy)
- Awareness of consequences

## Acceptable answers
- Mentions multiple options
- Explains why some were rejected
- Acknowledges imperfect decisions
- Understands downstream impact

## Red flags
- Only explains implementation
- No trade-offs mentioned
- “We followed best practices” without context
