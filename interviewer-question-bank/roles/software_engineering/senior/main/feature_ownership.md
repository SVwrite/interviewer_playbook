## Question
Tell me about a feature you owned end-to-end, from idea to production.
What were you responsible for?

## What this tests
- Sense of ownership
- Ability to drive work to completion
- Responsibility beyond coding

## Acceptable answers
- Covers requirements, implementation, testing, rollout
- Mentions coordination with others
- Talks about failures or follow-ups

## Red flags
- Only describes assigned tasks
- No production responsibility
- Avoids discussing problems
