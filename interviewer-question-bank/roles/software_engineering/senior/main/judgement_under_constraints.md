## Question
Tell me about a time you had to make a technical decision with incomplete information or tight constraints.
What did you do?

## What this tests
- Judgment under pressure
- Ability to balance ideal vs practical
- Risk assessment

## Acceptable answers
- Explains constraints clearly
- Describes reasoning process
- Accepts trade-offs knowingly

## Red flags
- Paralysis waiting for perfect information
- Reckless decisions without reasoning
- Blames constraints without ownership
