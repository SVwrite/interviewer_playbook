## Question
What are some common causes of production incidents that don’t show up in local testing?

## What this tests
- Operational thinking
- Experience beyond development environments

## Acceptable answers
- Mentions scale, data differences, concurrency, config issues
- Understands environment mismatch
- Mentions monitoring or alerting

## Red flags
- Vague answers
- Overreliance on “more testing”
