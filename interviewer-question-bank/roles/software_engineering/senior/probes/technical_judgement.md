## Question
Can you describe a technical decision you made that turned out to be suboptimal?  
What did you learn from it?

## What this tests
- Maturity
- Reflection and learning
- Ownership of mistakes

## Acceptable answers
- Honest discussion of a mistake
- Explains why the decision made sense at the time
- Mentions how behavior changed afterward

## Red flags
- “I can’t think of one”
- Blaming others
